**Heavy tree shade** in Alden Bridge, Cochran's Crossing, and Grogan's Mill means most yards are partial-sun at best. St. Augustine handles shade — Bermuda doesn't. If your Bermuda lawn is thinning under oak shade, the grass is wrong, not your mowing.
