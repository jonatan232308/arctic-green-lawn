## Landscape design in Tomball, TX — what you get

A landscape consultation is the planning step before any planting, hardscape, or bed work. We come to your yard, walk the property with you, and put together a plan that fits your space, your budget, and what you actually want to do with the yard.

What's included:

- **In-home walk-through** — typically 60–90 minutes
- **Site notes** — sun exposure, soil drainage, existing plants worth keeping, problem zones
- **Design recommendations** — bed shapes, plant choices for Gulf-Coast climate, focal points, hardscape options
- **Written plan + sketch** — what goes where, what it costs to install, phasing if you want to stage the work
- **Plant list with Tomball specifics** — what tolerates clay soil + August heat + winter freezes

The $150 consultation fee is **applied to the project cost** if you have us do the install. So if you decide we're the right fit, the design is effectively free.

## What Tomball yards actually need

Tomball runs a mix of older established subdivisions (Treeline, Inverness Estates) and newer acreage-style builds in Northpointe. Most homeowners we see want one of three things:

1. **Front-yard refresh** — beds got tired, original builder shrubs are oversized, want curb appeal back
2. **Backyard transformation** — empty Northpointe or Willow Creek backyard, ready to add patio + beds + shade trees
3. **Drought-tolerant redo** — tired of fighting the August dry spell, want a yard that survives without daily watering

Whatever you're after, we plan to **Gulf-Coast climate first**:

- **Heavy clay soil** — drains slowly, needs amended planting holes, no plants that hate wet feet
- **August heat + winter freezes** — both happen, plant choices have to handle both (we lost a lot of palms in the 2021 freeze — pick natives)
- **HOA approval** — Treeline, Inverness Estates, Northpointe all have varying landscape rules, we design to fit
- **Mature trees** — older Tomball lots often have big oaks and pines. We plan around root zones so new beds don't fight the shade or strangle the trees

## Tomball landscape design FAQ

### What does the $150 consultation actually include?

A walk-through of your yard with the lead designer (60–90 min), written notes on sun/drainage/soil, plant + bed recommendations specific to your lot, and a written plan with rough install cost. Applied to project total if we do the install.

### Can you design around HOA rules in Treeline or Northpointe?

Yes. We've designed to those HOAs before — we know the approved plant lists, hardscape limits, and submission process for both communities.

### What plants do well in Tomball, TX?

Native + adapted: live oaks, crape myrtles, dwarf yaupon, lantana, salvia, esperanza, ornamental grasses (muhly, gulf coast), Mexican plum. We avoid plants that flop in clay soil or can't handle a 25°F freeze.

### Do you handle full installation after the design?

Yes. Most clients have us do the install — bed prep, plant install, mulch, edging, irrigation tie-in. Hardscape (paver paths, stone features) handled in-house too.

### How long does a Tomball landscape project take?

A standard front-yard refresh runs 2–4 days install. A full backyard transformation with hardscape can take 1–3 weeks depending on scope. We give you a written timeline before any work starts.
