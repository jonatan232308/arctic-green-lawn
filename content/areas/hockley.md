## Hockley-specific care tips

**Sandy loam soil:** drains fast, dries faster. Deeper, less-frequent watering beats daily light watering — keeps roots reaching down.
**Pine straw:** rake at least twice a year. Builds up fast and smothers grass underneath.
**Front-line August heat:** mow at 3.5–4 inches in summer to shade roots.
