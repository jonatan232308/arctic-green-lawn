## Cy-Fair-specific care tips

**Heavy clay soil:** core aerate in spring before fertilizer goes down. Otherwise the granules sit on top and wash off in the next rain.
**Brown patch fungus:** common in fall. We flag it on every visit and recommend treatment if needed.
**Mulch + bed edging:** late February refresh. Keeps front beds tight through summer HOA cycles.
