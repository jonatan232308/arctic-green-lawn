## Cypress-specific care tips

**St. Augustine grass:** mow at 3.5–4 inches in summer to shade roots and hold moisture during August dry spells.
**Brown patch fungus:** common after fall rains — we look for it on every visit and flag treatment if needed.
**Mulch refresh:** late February before the heat sets in. Holds moisture and keeps weeds down through July.
