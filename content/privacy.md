# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 8, 2026

Arctic Green Lawn & Landscape LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can opt out at any time.

## Information we collect

When you submit our contact form at [arcticgreenlandscaping.com](https://arcticgreenlandscaping.com/contact/), we collect:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number** (optional)
- **Service interest** and **message** you provide

We also automatically log basic technical data — IP address, browser user agent, and submission timestamp — to detect spam and abuse.

## How we use your information

We use what you give us to:

1. Respond to your inquiry by phone, email, and/or text message
2. Schedule and confirm service appointments
3. Send service reminders and follow-ups for the consultation you requested
4. Improve our services and customer support

We do **not** sell your information. We do **not** share your phone number, email, or name with third parties for their marketing purposes.

## SMS / text messaging consent

By submitting our contact form with a phone number, you **consent to receive SMS / text messages** from Arctic Green Lawn & Landscape regarding your inquiry, including but not limited to:

- Confirmation that we received your request
- Appointment scheduling and reminders
- Follow-up communication about your free consultation
- Service updates related to your account

**Message frequency varies.** Message and data rates may apply per your wireless carrier's plan.

### How to opt out

You can opt out of SMS messages **at any time** by:

- **Replying STOP** to any text message from us — this immediately unsubscribes your number, and you will receive a confirmation reply.
- Other accepted opt-out keywords: STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, QUIT, END, REVOKE.

After opting out, we will **not** send further SMS messages to your number. To resubscribe, reply **START** or **YES** to a prior message thread, or submit our contact form again with consent.

### How to get help

Reply **HELP** to any text message and we will reply with our contact information and opt-out instructions. You can also call us at **(903) 658-8244** or email **agl1025@yahoo.com** for assistance.

## Phone calls

If you provide a phone number on our contact form, you consent to receive calls — including automated voice calls — from Arctic Green Lawn or a representative regarding your inquiry. All calls are made between **8:00 AM and 9:00 PM** in your local time zone, and you can request to be removed from our calling list at any time by:

- Telling our representative on a call to remove you
- Emailing **agl1025@yahoo.com** with your phone number and "Do Not Call"
- Replying STOP to any text message (this also removes you from our call list)

## Cookies and analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This tool sets cookies and may collect anonymized usage data such as page views, session length, and referrer source. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## Data retention

We keep contact form submissions and call records for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations (e.g., TCPA recordkeeping, typically 4 years). On opt-out request, we retain only the minimum data required to honor the opt-out (your phone number on our internal Do Not Contact list).

## Your rights

Texas residents and visitors have the right to:

- Know what personal information we have collected about them
- Request a copy of that information
- Request that we delete or correct that information
- Opt out of all future communications

To exercise any of these rights, email **agl1025@yahoo.com** with the subject line "Privacy Request" and we will respond within 30 days.

## Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be posted on this page; continued use of our website after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
