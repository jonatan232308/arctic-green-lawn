## A local crew, owner on every job

Arctic Green Lawn & Landscape is owner-operated by **Norbert Rodriguez** out of Northwest Houston, TX. We're not a franchise. We're not a faceless company that sends a different crew every week. When you call **(903) 658-8244**, you're calling Norbert.

We run weekly and bi-weekly maintenance routes through Cypress, Tomball, Spring, Katy, The Woodlands, Magnolia, Hockley, Waller, Jersey Village, Copperfield, Cy-Fair and Champions. Every truck is licensed, insured and trained in-house.

## What we believe

**Show up.** Most lawn care problems aren't technique problems. They're show-up problems. We run consistent routes so your yard gets cut on the day we said it would, every week — including the August weeks no one wants to be outside.

**Right, not cheap.** There's always somebody cheaper. The question is whether they'll still be answering the phone in July when the grass is two feet tall and the heat index is 105°. We will.

**Clean edges, blown walkways, every visit.** No shortcuts. The five extra minutes it takes to edge a walkway and blow off the driveway is the difference between a yard that looks mowed and a yard that looks taken care of.

## What we run

- Weekly and bi-weekly mowing on St. Augustine, Bermuda and Zoysia
- Spring and fall cleanups — leaves, pine straw, debris, bed refresh
- Landscape design and bed installation
- Tree and shrub trimming
- Irrigation system inspection and minor repair
- HOA-friendly maintenance schedules across master-planned NW Houston communities

## Who we work for

Homeowners and HOAs across Northwest Houston. From quarter-acre HOA lots in Copperfield to half-acre acreage cuts in Hockley, every yard gets the same standard.
