A well-placed walkway changes the whole feel of a yard. Instead of worn-out grass trails and mismatched stones, you get a finished path that guides the eye, handles foot traffic, and actually ages well.

## What we build

- **Paver walkways** — wide entry paths, side-yard walks, and garden connectors
- **Stepping-stone layouts** — modern spaced stones across lawns or gravel
- **Decorative borders** — river rock, cobble, or paver edging around flower beds
- **Rock features** — dry creek beds, boulder accents, and low-maintenance stone zones

## How it comes together

Every project starts with a free in-home walk-through. We look at grade, drainage, and how you actually move through the yard — then spec the pavers, base, and edging that will hold up through Texas heat and downpours.

Most walkways finish in 1–3 days. Bigger scopes (full front-yard redesign, stone-and-bed combos) we schedule like any landscape design project: design first, then install.

## Why homeowners love hardscape

- **Instant curb appeal** — a clean path reframes the entire front yard
- **Zero mowing** — replaces grass you didn't want to cut anyway
- **Lasts for decades** — proper base prep is the whole game, and we don't cut corners on it
- **Pairs with planting** — we handle the beds on either side in the same visit
