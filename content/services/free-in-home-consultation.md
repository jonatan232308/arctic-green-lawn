At Arctic Green Lawn & Landscaping, we believe in understanding your vision before starting any project. Our free in-home consultation is designed to provide you with expert advice and personalized solutions for your outdoor space.

## What to Expect

**Initial Meeting:** Our team visits your home to discuss your ideas and goals.

**Expert Advice:** We offer suggestions and innovative ideas tailored to your needs.

**Project Planning:** For design projects, we provide a detailed design and quote. If the project moves forward, the design fee is waived.

**Quote Approval:** For non-design projects, you receive a quote within 24-48 hours.

**Scheduling:** Upon approval, we schedule the project start date, collecting a deposit if necessary.
