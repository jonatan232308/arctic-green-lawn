# Voice — Arctic Green

Tone we write in. Used by `/service-page` and `/blog-post` skills.

## Anchor

We are the **non-corporate alternative** to TruGreen and Weed Man in NW Houston. Owner-operated by Norbert Rodriguez. Crew shows up, does the work, leaves the lawn looking sharp. No upsell pressure.

## Sentence rhythm

Short. Direct. One idea per sentence. Mix in occasional longer sentence for cadence — but never two long sentences back-to-back.

## Vocabulary

- "yard" not "lawn surface"
- "crew" not "technicians"
- "we" not "Arctic Green Lawn & Landscape, LLC"
- "show up" not "arrive at the scheduled appointment"
- "fix" not "remediate"

## What we never sound like

- Corporate brochure ("In today's fast-paced world...")
- AI slop ("comprehensive guide to everything you need to know about...")
- Over-promising ("guaranteed perfect lawn forever")
- Generic stock copy from competitors

## Voice contrast — bad → good

**Bad**: "Our comprehensive lawn maintenance service ensures the optimal aesthetic appeal of your residential property throughout all seasons."

**Good**: "We mow your yard every week. Edges crisp, walkways blown off, gate latched. That's it."

**Bad**: "It is recommended to schedule a consultation at your earliest convenience to discuss your unique landscaping requirements."

**Good**: "Call us. We come look at it. You get a price the same week."

## Trigger phrases that mean STOP rewrite

If a draft uses any of these, scrap and rewrite:
- "in today's fast-paced world"
- "comprehensive guide"
- "everything you need to know about"
- "ensure optimal"
- "leverage our expertise"
- "tailored solutions"
- "at your earliest convenience"
- "trusted partner"
